About me

 

I am currently working as a production manager and quality control manager at Allied Painters Inc. We paint everything from old houses to new houses to industrial buildings and everything inbetween. A typical day for me would consist of driving anywhere from Logan, UT to Payson, UT.

Long before I was born, my dad was getting tired of working temporary jobs and came up with a plan. He made a trip to the library and found a couple of books about computers. He dove right in and realized he had a knack for this kind of stuff. After reading all the books he started taking tests and getting certified in all different areas of the computing world. He eventually got a job as a programmer and has been happy in his career ever since.

Growing up, my dad was able to build a lot of cool things using his extensive knowledge. It sparked something in me and coding is something that I have been wanting to learn since I was around the age of 14. I first tried learning java but that is a big mistake for a beginner. It kind of turned me off to the programming world and I forgot about it. But when I learned about all the cool stuff you could do with just HTML and CSS, I was hooked.
